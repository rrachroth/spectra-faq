Spectra Q&A

What is Spectra?
Spectra is a music streaming app that lets users switch between different versions of a song, like Regular, Live Concert, and Up Close & Raw.
How does the Discover function work?
The Discover section includes a For You page with personalized recommendations and a Following section to keep up with favorite artists.
Is Spectra free to use?
Spectra will offer both free and premium subscription options, allowing users to explore different features.
